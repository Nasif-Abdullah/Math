ZF (without urelements) rigorously construct natural numbers, from which the integers, rational numbers and real numbers are constructed. We will discuss the latter constructions in our real analysis notes, preferably in chapter one.

## Von-Neumann Construction of Naturals
The Natural numbers is the infinite set $$\mathbb{N} = {1,2,3,....}$$ which is crucial for counting, designing sequences etc. In ZF, the natural numbers are created from the empty set, in a process called von-neumann construction. We use no urelements in ZF (commonly known as thr Zermelo-Fraenkel Set theory).

### 
Our universe begins with the empty set, $$\emptyset$$.
